Welcome to BEE-DALAT

Welcome to the BEE-DALAT Business Club, where we inspire and equip high school students at Dalat International School to become future leaders in business. Here, we blend entrepreneurship with Christian values, fostering an environment where students can learn, grow, and give back.

About Us

BEE-DALAT stands for Business Education and Entrepreneurship at Dalat, a club exclusively for the students of Dalat International School. Our club is a place where young minds come together to explore the world of business from both a practical and ethical perspective. We offer workshops, mentorship programs, and opportunities to start small ventures under guidance.

Our Mission

Our mission at BEE-DALAT is twofold:

  • To mentor and prepare high school students for real-world business challenges by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to start their own ventures.
  • To integrate Biblical principles into business education, encouraging a model of entrepreneurship that prioritizes giving back to the community and using business as a means to help the less fortunate.
We believe that true success in business involves not just financial gain but also making a positive impact on others' lives.

Join Us

Are you passionate about business and looking to make a difference? Join BEE-DALAT today! Whether you're interested in learning about entrepreneurship, seeking mentorship, or eager to start your own project, we're here to support you on your journey. Let's work together to create businesses that not only succeed but also serve.
